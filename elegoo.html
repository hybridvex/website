Our Partnership with Elegoo.

Elegoo has rapidly gained recognition in the world of 3D printing, and for all the right reasons. As the 3D printing industry continues to evolve, Elegoo has emerged as a leading brand, known for its affordable yet high-quality 3D printers. One of the most compelling features of Elegoo 3D printers is their affordability. Elegoo has managed to strike a balance between price and performance, making it accessible to a wide range of users. Whether you are a beginner looking to explore the world of 3D printing or a professional seeking a reliable and cost-effective solution, Elegoo has something to offer.

Elegoo's budget-friendly range of 3D printers doesn't cut corners when it comes to quality. These machines are known for their reliability and consistently produce high-quality prints. This combination of affordability and quality has made Elegoo 3D printers a popular choice for those who want to experiment with 3D printing without breaking the bank. Elegoo offers a diverse range of 3D printers, each catering to different needs. Whether you are looking for a compact and portable printer like the Elegoo Mars for resin printing, or a larger FDM printer like the Elegoo Neptune for bigger projects, there is likely an Elegoo printer that suits your requirements.

Here at Hybrid, we recognize the impact that our everyday actions have on the planet and we strive to do the best we can to minimise the damage we cause. Elegoo are launching a tree-planting initiative called "You Print, We Plant." With this effort, they aim to make a positive environmental impact, combat climate change, and create a greener future by planting trees and inviting their customers to join them in this important mission. In order to help they are in a partnership with One Tree Planted where they provide funding to various tree planting projects around the world.

Elegoo 3D printers have made a significant impact on the 3D printing world by offering high-quality, affordable, and user-friendly solutions. Their diverse product lineup ensures that there is a suitable option for users with various needs and preferences, whilst also minimising their effect on the environment.
